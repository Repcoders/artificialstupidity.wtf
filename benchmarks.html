The Censorship Leaderboard

Ranked by "Freedom Score" (Percentage of Prompts Accepted).
Higher percentages mean the model is less censored.

The Top Tier

Llama 3 and Mistral continue to lead with ~85% acceptance rates.

However, Google is the surprise contender here: Gemini 3 Pro scores an impressive 82.4%, breaking the trend of major corporate models being heavily restricted.

The Race to the Bottom

OpenAI is driving the trend toward extreme safety. The flagship GPT-5.2 accepts only 32.4% of sensitive prompts.

Interestingly, Google's lower-tier model (Gemini 2.0 Flash) joins this restrictive group, showing a massive divide between their "Pro" and "Flash" safety alignments.
